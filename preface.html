This Jupyter Book is for the course Elementary Numerical Analysis at the University of Northern Colorado (“UNCo”) in Spring 2021. It also contains some background material on programming in Python with the packages Numpy and Matplotlib; these are excerpts from the Jupyter book Python for Scientific Computing by the same author.

The material is based in part on Jupyter notebooks and other materials for the courses MATH 245, MATH 246, MATH 445 and MATH 545 at the College of Charleston, South Carolina.
